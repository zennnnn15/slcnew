Financing Options / PAG-IBIG Guide

Helping you navigate your dream home financing through PAG-IBIG Fund.

Who Qualifies?

PAG-IBIG members with at least 24 monthly savings, not more than 65 years old at loan application, and with legal capacity to acquire and encumber real property.

Step-by-Step PAG-IBIG Loan Process

  1. Complete loan application form and requirements
  2. Submit documents to nearest PAG-IBIG branch
  3. Loan evaluation and property appraisal
  4. Loan approval and signing of loan documents
  5. Loan release and start of amortization payments

Requirements Checklist

  • Duly accomplished PAG-IBIG Housing Loan Application Form
  • Proof of income (payslips, ITR, or Certificate of Employment)
  • Two valid government-issued IDs
  • TIN or Tax Identification Number
  • Marriage Certificate (if applicable)
  • Authorization to conduct credit background check
